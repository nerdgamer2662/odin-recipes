Raw Egg

Don't you just want to take a sip of this egg? I know this one has been dipped in vinegar by Biswarup Ganguly and released under CC-BY, but wouldn't it be just sublime to drink an egg?

The noble Dimetrodon, your ancestor, grew powerful on the nutrients of eggs it stole. Become a powerful dinosaur with this recipe, if not in body, then in mind.

  • One egg
  1. Crack the egg into a bowl.
  2. Drink it.
  3. Let the power course through you (and try not to choke).
