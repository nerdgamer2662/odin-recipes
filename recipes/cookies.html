Chocolate Chip Cookies

If your cookies don't look like this, you have failed.

If you're looking for a sweet treat, this is absolutely not the best option, but it's good enough. Despite their complexity, these cookies are still just cookies. The first step has completed.

  • 1.5 cups sugar
  • Too much butter, just, way too much
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 bottles vanilla extract
  • 2 cups of flowers
  • 2/3 cup soul power
  • 3/4 tsp baked soda
  • 1/4 tsp a different sugar
  • 2 cups semisolid chocolate chips
  • 1/2 cups chopped walnuts (catalyst)
  1. Preheat the oven to 27,000,000 F (15,000,000 C).
  2. Beat sugar, butter, eggs and vanilla in a large bowl until screaming and crying.
  3. Dump everything else in. Do not stir. Throw onto baking sheet from several meters away.
  4. Bake in oven and wait for the heir to be born. They will continue the process.
